12-Week Training Plan
5k → 10k · Epsom · 12 weeks
⚡ How this works
Each week has 3 runs. Do all 3 before moving to the next week — on whichever days work for you. Space them out with at least one rest day between hard sessions (intervals, tempo, long run). Your 10k goal pace is already within reach; this plan builds the distance and the speed to get there.

⚡ End of Phase 1 — What You Should Feel
Easy runs feel genuinely easy at 6'30"–7'00"/km with HR below 145. Tempo at 5'30"–5'45"/km feels controlled, not desperate. 5k time trial under 29 minutes. Long run hits 7.5km without wiping you out. This aerobic base is what makes Phase 2 speed work actually land.
